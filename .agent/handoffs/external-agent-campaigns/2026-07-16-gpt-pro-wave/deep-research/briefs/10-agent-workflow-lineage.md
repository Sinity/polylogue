Title: "[research 10] AI-agent workflow, task, and lineage evidence models"

Job ID: `deep-research-10`

Research interoperable models of multi-agent coding work without collapsing a
workflow run, lane/task, agent session, attempt, model invocation, artifact,
commit, PR, and Bead effect into one identity. Examine current telemetry,
provenance, workflow standards, and agent traces; cover forks, resumes,
subagents, retries, partial outputs, conflicting claims, and late evidence.

Map conclusions to `1vpm.6`, `2qx.2`, `4ts`, `t8t`, and `z9gh.7`. Assess the
archive unit `workflow run → lane/task → sessions → commits/PRs/Beads effects`
and deliver a minimal evidence/edge taxonomy with uncertainty and falsifying
fixtures—not a proposal to replace Polylogue with an external standard.
