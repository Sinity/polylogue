Title: "[research 11] Privacy-safe structural inference from conversational archives"

Job ID: `deep-research-11`

Research privacy-preserving derivation of shareable structural schemas,
workload profiles, and synthetic data from private conversation archives. Cover
leakage through keys, rare strings, quasi-identifiers, nested payloads and
metadata; distinguish useful DP from practical allowlists, generalization,
thresholds, and release review. Seek failures where schema inference turns
observed text into public property names.

Map results to `1xc.14.1.2`, `.1.3`, and `.1`. Recommend an enforceable
classification/release contract that distinguishes harmless operational
metadata from sensitive content, plus operator-review surfaces and a local
red-team corpus that could falsify it.
