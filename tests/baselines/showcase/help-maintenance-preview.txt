Usage: polylogue maintenance preview [OPTIONS]

  Staleness inventory by model and scope. Read-only.

  Shows per-model counts of stale/missing/orphan rows with typed
  :class:`InvalidationReason` tags. Use before triggering ``polylogue
  maintenance run`` so the operator knows what will be rebuilt and why. Models
  with nothing stale produce explicit zero rows rather than being absent from
  the output.

Options:
  --scope [derived|retrieval|archive_cleanup|backfill]
                                  Limit preview to named scopes (derived,
                                  retrieval, archive_cleanup, backfill).
  --output-format [plain|json]    Output format.  [default: plain]
  --shallow                       Skip the expensive full-verification path
                                  (faster, slightly less accurate).
  -h, --help                      Show this message and exit.
