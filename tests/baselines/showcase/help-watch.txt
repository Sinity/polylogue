Usage: polylogue watch [OPTIONS]

  Watch source directories and ingest new sessions live.

Options:
  --root PATH         Override watch root (repeatable). Defaults to
                      ~/.claude/projects and ~/.codex/sessions.
  --debounce-s FLOAT  Quiet-period (seconds) before parsing a modified file.
                      [default: 2.0]
  -h, --help          Show this message and exit.
