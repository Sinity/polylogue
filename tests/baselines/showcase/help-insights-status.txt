Usage: polylogue insights status [OPTIONS]

  Report insight materialization coverage and readiness.

Options:
  --insight TEXT       Insight readiness target. May be repeated.
  -p, --provider TEXT  Limit provider coverage details to one provider.
  --since TEXT         Limit coverage details to rows at/after this timestamp
                       or date.
  --until TEXT         Limit coverage details to rows at/before this timestamp
                       or date.
  -f, --format [json]  Output format.
  -h, --help           Show this message and exit.
