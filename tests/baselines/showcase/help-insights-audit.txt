Usage: polylogue insights audit [OPTIONS]

  Report per-product rigor profile across materialized insights (#1275).

  For each contracted product, reports the share of rows that carry an
  evidence payload, an inference payload, and a fallback marker, plus the
  stale-version row count and a confidence-bucket distribution.

Options:
  --insight TEXT          Limit the audit to one or more registered insight
                          names. Default: every contracted product.
  --sample-limit INTEGER  Maximum rows per product to sample for the rigor
                          profile.  [default: 500]
  -f, --format [json]     Output format.
  -h, --help              Show this message and exit.
